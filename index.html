Steve Jobs

His achievements revolutionised the world we live in today.

Steve Jobs Profile

Born:25 February 1955.
Died:5 October 2011.
Early life:Born in San Francisco, Jobs was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs as a baby and soon moved to Mountain View in Silicon Valley.

Jobs and Wozniak
On a fateful day in 1969, a 14-year-old Steve Jobs met Steve Wozniak, then 19.
The pair bonded over a love of electronics, and went on to design and build the first Apple computer in the Jobs family garage.

Seeking enlightenment
Jobs dropped out of college in 1972, travelled to India and came back a Buddhist.

Jobs and Apple
After building the Apple 1 in 1976, pre-orders made it possible to develop and build an Apple II the following year.

The Apple company was soon founded and, in 1980, was taken public. Steve Jobs’ net worth rose to over $200m.

A power struggle meant Mr Jobs was ousted from the Apple in 1985. After leaving the company he founded computer developer NeXT and Pixar.
In 1997, Apple acquired NeXT and Mr Jobs became Apple CEO. He is credited with rescuing the company from the brink of bankruptcy with an array of innovative new products.
